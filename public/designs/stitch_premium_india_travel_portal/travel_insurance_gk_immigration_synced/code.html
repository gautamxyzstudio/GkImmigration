Frequently Asked Questions

Is travel insurance mandatory for my visa? expand_more

Yes, for many destinations like Schengen countries, travel insurance with a minimum €30,000 coverage is mandatory for visa approval.

What does the medical coverage include? expand_more

It typically includes inpatient/outpatient treatment, emergency medical evacuation, and repatriation of remains.

How much does a typical plan cost? expand_more

Costs depend on age, duration, and destination. Basic plans start as low as ₹49 per day for certain domestic journeys.

Can I buy insurance at the last minute? expand_more

Absolutely! Our platform allows you to purchase insurance and receive your policy via email even minutes before you check in.
